Since I was a kid, I have loved space. The vastness of it alone blew my mind - so big that I couldn't write down it's size with all of the pencils. To tiny me, the universe was full of possibilites - that anything I could dream up might exist. It felt very cool that I was apart of it even though I knew I would never be able to fully understand it.

Being an astronaut was never going to happen. I'm scared of heights, and the dark, and I have bad eyes. Luckily the internet holds the same inifite wonder! Plus I get to stay on the ground, my screen lights up, and it would almost be weird if a programmer didn't wear glasses.

I sincerely love being apart of something so unknown and undefined. The internet in 20 years will be unrecognizable to us today. I am dedicated to learning as many new languages and technologies as possible so that I may have a tiny fraction of influence on what the internet will become.
